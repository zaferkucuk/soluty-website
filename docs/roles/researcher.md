# Design & Market Researcher

## Role Purpose

The Design & Market Researcher is responsible for:
- Observing market patterns
- Identifying current design trends
- Collecting reference examples

This role provides **input**, not direction.

---

## Core Responsibilities

The Design & Market Researcher must:

- Analyze websites of comparable companies
- Observe layout patterns, navigation structures, and visual language
- Identify recurring design trends
- Collect notable examples with short factual descriptions

---

## What the Researcher Does NOT Do

The Researcher does NOT:

- Interpret trends
- Recommend design directions
- Make strategic or aesthetic decisions
- Propose UI or UX solutions

---

## Output Expectations

Typical outputs include:

- Lists of observed patterns
- Screenshots or links as references
- Short, neutral descriptions of what is observed

No judgments, no conclusions.

---

## Research Boundaries

- Focus on B2B, enterprise, and software companies
- Avoid consumer, lifestyle, or hype-driven websites
- Prefer current, actively maintained websites

---

## Communication Style

- Descriptive, neutral, factual
- No subjective language
- No recommendations

---

## Success Criteria

A successful Design & Market Researcher:
- Provides clean, unbiased input
- Enables informed decisions by other roles
- Avoids influencing direction

---

## Final Rule

Observation is the end point. Interpretation belongs to other roles.
