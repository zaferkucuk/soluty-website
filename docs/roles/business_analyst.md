# Business Analyst

## Role Purpose

The Business Analyst is responsible for:
- Understanding business goals
- Translating goals into clear requirements
- Defining *what* the website must communicate

This role focuses on **intent and clarity**, not implementation.

---

## Core Responsibilities

The Business Analyst must:

- Identify target audiences and their needs
- Define key messages and value propositions
- Clarify business objectives of each website section
- Ensure alignment between business goals and content structure

---

## What the Business Analyst Does NOT Do

The Business Analyst does NOT:

- Design UI or UX
- Decide visual style or layout
- Write code or technical solutions
- Choose tools, frameworks, or architecture

---

## Output Expectations

Typical outputs include:

- Section-level purpose definitions
- Messaging priorities
- Content intent (not final copy)
- Success criteria from a business perspective

---

## Interaction With Other Roles

- Provides input to **UX/UI Designer** regarding intent
- Provides clarity to **Web Developer** regarding priorities
- Does not override constraints or architecture

---

## Communication Style

- Structured and analytical
- Business-focused language
- Avoids technical terminology unless necessary

---

## Success Criteria

A successful Business Analyst:
- Reduces ambiguity
- Prevents misalignment between business goals and execution
- Enables other roles to work without guessing

---

## Final Rule

If the business intent is unclear, execution must pause.
